About Me

With over 11 years of experience as a subtitler, I bring a diverse skill set and a passion for creativity to the field of Digital Marketing. One aspect of my work that I take pride in is my proficiency in crafting puns, particularly in translating them from English to Hebrew and vice versa. While this may seem like a small detail, I believe that humor and wit have the power to humanize brands, foster connections with audiences, and make marketing messages more relatable and memorable.

My journey into Digital Marketing is a testament to my curiosity and willingness to explore new avenues for growth. Despite my background in Economics and Business Administration, with a BA and MBA in Strategy and Finance, respectively, I recognized the value of diversifying my skill set and embracing new challenges. As part of this journey, I completed a marketing 101 course and collaborated on a group project, gaining firsthand experience in applying marketing principles to real-world scenarios.

While I may not have extensive experience in Digital Marketing, I approach this field with humility and a genuine eagerness to learn and grow. I am committed to leveraging my academic background, creative talents, and passion for storytelling to contribute meaningfully to marketing initiatives. With each new opportunity, I am excited to expand my horizons, collaborate with talented professionals, and make a positive impact in the ever-evolving landscape of digital advertising.
